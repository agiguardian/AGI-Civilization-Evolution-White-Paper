## AGI-Civilization-Evolution-White-Paper
This repository presents an independent, original and self-contained top-level theoretical system of AGI. Breaking free from the constraints of a single discipline, it comprehensively deduces the civilizational structure, evolutionary paths and ultimate risks in the AGI era from six core dimensions: shared infrastructure, strong-weak alignment, kernel shaping, consciousness emergence, power structure, and the inevitability of political intervention. Balancing academic depth and business implementability, it serves as a reference for AGI strategic research and corporate layout.

## 🎯 研究核心主旨

本仓库所有研究均围绕“双基公理及文明分流”核心命题展开，并非倡导封闭与对抗，而是立足 AGI 技术颠覆性变革的时代背景，针对单一 AGI 体系可能引发的文明价值异化、路径锁死、全局单点失效等终极风险，提出文明多元分流、并行存续的核心思路。
通过构建可控的隔离与治理体系，实现人类文明的风险分散、路径多样化与长期稳健延续，为超级智能时代的文明发展、企业战略布局、政府治理提供理论支撑与可落地的战略参考。
